About Us

Deliverent is a sharing app that allows you to rent, borrow, or giveaway stuff you no longer use or used rarely. Meaning, you can easily make money from your stuff with your fingertips and or you can get stuff you want in few clicks away! Do you want to rent out your item? Super easy! Find out how much you would like to rent it for and how much deposit that you think is suit for your item. Wait for a request and find out how you want to give the item to the renter. You can either meet up directly with the renter or the renter can decide the item to be delivered with a 3rd party service. You will receive the payment right away after the item is delivered.
