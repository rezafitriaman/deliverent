About Us

Deliverent is a sharing app that allows you to rent, borrow, or giveaway stuff you no longer use or used rarely. Meaning, you can easily make money from your stuff with your fingertips and or you can get stuff you want in few clicks away!

Do you want to rent out your item? Super easy! Find out how much you would like to rent it for and how much deposit that you think is suit for your item. Wait for a request and find out how you want to give the item to the renter. You can either meet up directly with the renter or the renter can decide the item to be delivered with a 3rd party service. You will receive the payment right away after the item is delivered. Don’t worry about any damage, your item is secured and safe with your deposit that will be keep for you until your item is back in safe condition.

Going for holiday and need to rent an item? Find your item that you need in the app from search box or browse a list of items on the app from our categories. Pick the best item for you and contact the renter using our chat service. Pay securely using our secure payment service online with no extra cost!

Items to borrow; books, games equipment, educational-related stuff, etc.

Items to giveaway; unused clothes, books, things that you want to get rid of but it still may still have value for other people. Consider this is your personal way to do charity!

What is Deliverent?

Deliverent is a sharing app that allows you to rent, borrow and give away stuff you no longer use or used rarely. Meaning, you can easily make money with your stuff with your fingertips! On the other hand, if there’s stuff you need for your last minute holiday or for an event, you can get stuff you want in a few clicks away!

How to post your item for rent/borrow/free?

You can simply login or sign up to Deliverent App

  • go to homepage
  • click + button and decide if you want to rent, borrow, or give away your stuff
  • upload photo of your item
  • decide on how much deposit and daily rent you want
  • fill your item’s availability and description
  • decide how to deliver the item
  • click save!
How to order an item for rent/borrow/free?

You can simply login or sign up to Deliverent App

  • go to homepage
  • find item you want to rent/borrow/get for free
  • check availability of the item
  • if you need to get more information, you can chat the item’s owner directly in the app
  • decide on how you want the item to be delivered
  • make payment! – make sure you have enough money in your Deliverent Virtual Account
How to top up your money into your Deliverent Virtual Account?

You can simply login or sign up to Deliverent App

  • Click navigation menu on top left
  • Click + button on deposit
  • Decide how much you want to top up
  • Choose your transaction via ATM, KlikBCA, or M-BCA
  • Pay now!
